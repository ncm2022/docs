## **Offline Resources**

1. **Comprehensive Courseware**
   You will have access to our courseware, which is designed in a workbook style. This style makes it easy for you to take organized notes and engage in practice exercises for each chapter. To know more, refer [here](https://app.notion.com/p/Learning-Element-Courseware-16178960cbe64a248d0b2d1535133791?pvs=21).
2. **Comprehensive Classroom Sessions**
   Our classroom sessions are aligned with your practice materials, helping you develop a strong conceptual understanding of the subject matter.
3. **In-Class test**
   You will benefit from chapter-wise tests that ensure continuous learning and allow you to assess your progress regularly.
4. **One-to-One Personal Mentoring**\
   You will receive personalized support from a dedicated mentor who will help you create a custom study plan, analyze your SimTest results, suggest strategies to improve your scores and identify potential universities to which you can apply. Depending on your preference, these mentoring sessions can be conducted offline or online. To know more, refer [here](https://app.notion.com/p/Learning-Element-myPlan-6b1859cd20ba468dbc5996ce9285b7c3?pvs=21).
5. **Masterclasses**\
   In our masterclasses, you will engage in sessions that cover replicas and past-year questions, participate in live mock solving, and prepare thoroughly for your final exams. To know more, refer [here](https://app.notion.com/p/Learning-Element-Masterclasses-8c83b46072b74288b472e7c93e552caa?pvs=21).
6. **Comprehensive WAT-PI**\
   To ensure you are well-prepared for the next round, **y**ou will receive guidance in writing your statement of purpose (SOP), reviewing your essays, and participating in mock personal interviews (PIs).
   We’re here to support you every step of the way!

## **Online Resources**

1. **Pre-class Videos**\
   With approximately 80 hours of videos to kickstart your understanding of the concepts covered in class, you'll have the chance to grasp these ideas better and enhance your participation during class discussions.
2. **Topic-wise Benchmarking Tests**\
   These benchmarking tests allow you to evaluate your knowledge and application of the concepts in a limited-time online test setting. It's a great way to see where you stand!
3. **Practice Exercise Video Solutions**\
   All practice exercises come with video solutions for over 2000 questions, ensuring you can instantly resolve doubts. These video solutions will teach you the best shortcuts, tricks, and advanced applications in an easy-to-understand format.
4. **Module Test**\
   Module Tests are conducted after each Module is completed in class. They are checkpoints that allow students to test their learning within each Module with subjects tested together. There are 9 Module Tests, all with video solutions, as it ensures that students can verify their answers and methods while learning the best shortcuts and tricks after completing the tests.
5. **SimTests**\
   Challenge yourself with 145 IPM BBA and 20 IPMAT Indore full-length mock tests that cover 24 different IPM and BBA entrance exams. This will help you benchmark your preparation against others. To know more, refer [here](https://app.notion.com/p/Learning-Element-Tests-bab1c36e7fe3466681952294cc202cf7?pvs=21).
6. **GK Zone**\
   Stay updated with concise GK notes, weekly current affairs, and exam-wise compendiums- all accompanied by quizzes to reinforce your learning.
   Engage with these resources to maximize your success! Remember to keep your password private and do not share it with anyone.
