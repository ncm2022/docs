**myIMS is our student portal**.

**How to log in to the myIMS portal?:**

Follow the below 3-step process if you are logging in to the student portal for the very first time.

1. Enter your IMS PIN in the IMS Student Portal [https://myimsv3.imsindia.com/](https://myimsv3.imsindia.com/) _(check your mail for the IMS PIN)_
2. Check your Inbox or your SMS for OTP and enter it in the box provided
3. Create a new Password. Do not share your password with anyone.

_If you encounter any issues while logging in, you can raise a ticket by [clicking here](https://support.imsindia.com/support/tickets/new)._

myIMS can also be accessed on your mobile via our apps:

- [Download the Android version here](https://tinyurl.com/yckyst9v)
- [Download the iOS version here](https://apps.apple.com/in/app/myims-3-0-2024-version/id1660922261)

⚠️ ⚠️ In case you only see the Home tab, it’s possible that you have been enrolled in multiple programs, and you need to select the appropriate program to explore further. Kindly choose your enrolled course from the "**Change Program**" option located at the top right corner in your myims portal.
