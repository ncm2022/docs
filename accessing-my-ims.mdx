title: "Accessing myIMS"
---

Welcome to myIMS, your essential student portal for Law preparation! Here’s how to access it for the first time:

1. **Visit the Student Portal:** Enter your IMS PIN to the myIMS Student Portal.
2. **Reset Your Password:** Click “Forgot Password” to initiate a reset.
3. **Enter Your Details:** Provide your registered email ID or IMS PIN to get your OTP.
4. **Check for OTP:** Look for the OTP in your email or SMS.
5. **Create a New Password:** Enter the OTP and set a new password. Remember to keep your password secure and do not share it with anyone.

You can also access myIMS on your mobile device by downloading our app:

- Download the Android version here

- Download the iOS version here

<Warning>
  If you only see the “Home” tab upon logging in, you might be enrolled in multiple programs!
</Warning>

<Note>
  Click on the “Change Program” option at the top right corner to explore the right program for you.
</Note>
