## Home

As you login to the myIMS student portal, you will see the below features on the Home page.

- **Notifications:** A sliding panel that notifies you about important events at any time
- **Notice Board:** List of all important Events and Updates curated for IMS students
- **Calendar:** List of upcoming sessions, events, and tests
- **Blogs:** List of important blogs for your test preparation journey

Under Channel, we upload all the video content created for your learning. We present the following content to broaden your learning:

- **One to Many Mentorship Sessions:** Listen and learn from IMS experts about what it takes to crack various competitive exams for MBA such as CAT, XAT, NMAT, SNAP, and CET.
- **Future You:** Hear from experts from various industries who have gone through the same journey as you. They discuss their careers and how they achieved their goals.
- **The B-School Insider:** Each session is an in-depth discussion with students currently attending elite B-Schools in India.
- **SimCAT Strategy & Analysis:** Learn how to analyze SimCATs in the best possible manner
- **Special Sessions:** Any other special sessions that we do for our students will be visible here.

Our in-house experts write on a variety of topics ranging from “How to lose the fear of Math” to “Mastering Set Theory”.

**Our blogs are: The CAT Writer, CAT-holics, CET Blog**

## Prepare

This is crucial for your study journey. It consists of three sections listed below.

- **Guides:** Learn about the exam you want to crack and set your targets
- **GK:** Include a weekly dose of Current Affairs in your test preparation along with conceptual knowledge and you are preparing yourself to face off the best in this country.
- [\*\*Modules](Learning%20Element%20-%20Concept%20Learning%20&%20Practice%2027401bbfeecf8162bb78ec03bb4c8168.md):\*\* Your core conceptual learning starts here.

## Test

[Under this tab](Learning%20Element%20-%20Tests%2027401bbfeecf81b9bd76d8686736d536.md) all the Tests that you need to take in order to be exam ready are housed. SimCATs, ADMATs, Module Tests, Sectional Tests and Past Year CAT papers are uploaded here.

## MyPage

- **Bookmarks** - This section displays all the items you have bookmarked.
- **myTasks** - Here, you will find anything you’ve added to your tasks.
- [**myPlans**](Learning%20Element%20-%20Mentoring%20and%20Guidance%2027401bbfeecf8159bd9fd0505e12e4a8.md) - This section includes one-on-one mentorship sessions with IMS mentors.

## Performance Tab

Under this tab you will get detailed analytics of the tests you have attempted.

- **Score Summary** - You can check the score summary of all the tests you have given.
- **Percentile Predictor** - Based on the scores from at least 3 SimCATs you have taken, this gives you the possible percentile you could get in the actual exam
- **Question Selection** - You check your question selection strategy topic wise, section wise and test wise.
- **Progress** **Tracker** - This helps you monitor your progress across different tests
