---
title: "Accessing myIMS"
---

You can access the myIMS platform either **through your web browser or via their mobile app using your registered credentials**. \[[1](https://myimsv3.imsindia.com/), [2](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.india.ims.imsindia3&hl=en_IN), [3](https://support.imsindia.com/support/solutions/articles/81000416570-how-do-i-start-utilizing-my-enrolled-course-)\]

**1. Access via Web Browser**

- Visit the [**<u>myIMS Student Portal</u>**](https://myimsv3.imsindia.com/). \[[1](https://support.imsindia.com/support/solutions/articles/81000416570-how-do-i-start-utilizing-my-enrolled-course-)\]
- **If you are logging in for the first time:**
  1. Enter your IMS PIN (provided upon enrollment).
  2. Click on **Forgot Password**.
  3. Enter your registered email ID or IMS PIN to receive an OTP via email or SMS.
  4. Input the OTP and create a new password. \[[1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=01o5PK9ZuyE&t=5), [2](https://support.imsindia.com/support/solutions/articles/81000416570-how-do-i-start-utilizing-my-enrolled-course-)\]
- **For returning logins:** Simply use your mobile number or IMS PIN alongside your created password. \[[1](https://support.imsindia.com/support/solutions/articles/81000416570-how-do-i-start-utilizing-my-enrolled-course-), [2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=01o5PK9ZuyE&t=5)\]

**2. Access via Mobile App**

- Download the **myIMS 3.0** application from the [**<u>Google Play Store</u>**](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.india.ims.imsindia3&hl=en_IN) or the App Store on iOS.
- Log in using the same credentials (IMS PIN/Registered Mobile Number and Password) for full on-the-go access to your course modules, class schedules, and live sessions. \[[1](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.india.ims.imsindia3&hl=en_IN), [3](https://support.imsindia.com/support/solutions/articles/81000419258-how-can-i-access-or-join-my-batch-live-class-), [4](https://support.imsindia.com/support/solutions/articles/81000416616-how-can-i-access-or-join-my-batch-s-live-class-)\]

**Navigating the Platform**

Once logged in, you will find several key tabs at the top of the screen:

- **Home:** View upcoming live classes (under the **My Channels** section), notice boards, and test deadlines.
- **Prepare:** Access study modules, pre-class videos, and practice exercises.
- **Test:** Take module tests and SIMCATs.
- **Performance:** Track your score summaries, percentiles, and progress. \[[1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b_7UvYxUx5g&t=3), [2](https://support.imsindia.com/support/solutions/articles/81000416616-how-can-i-access-or-join-my-batch-s-live-class-)\]
