## **Comprehensive Courseware (Books)**

Within 7 working days of enrollment, you will have access to our comprehensive courseware, which is designed in such a way that the whole syllabus is composed into manageable modules.

| **Sr. No.** | **Details of Courseware (Books)** |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | CATapult Courseware - Grammar Module |
| 2 | CATapult Courseware - WordList |
| 3 | CATapult Courseware - Module 0 |
| 4 | CATapult Courseware - Module 1 |
| 5 | CATapult Courseware - Module 2 |
| 6 | CATapult Courseware - Module 3 |
| 7 | CATapult Courseware - Module 4 |
| 8 | CATapult Courseware - Module 5 |

- **Grammar and Wordlist Modules:** A strong grasp of basic concepts will improve your performance on the Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension sections and help in every test.
- **Module 0:** This module serves as a refresher and introduction to key foundational topics, preparing you for deeper dives in later modules. On the myIMS portal, we have provided videos of our expert mentors solving all the questions from Module 0.
- **The Modules: 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5**
  - Modules 1 to 4 focus solely on the Learning and Practice required for the five areas tested on the CAT and other MBA exams – Quantitative Ability (QA), Verbal Ability (VA), Reading Comprehension (RC), Data Interpretation (DI), and Logical Reasoning (LR).
  - Every topic in each module will have Theory followed by Concept builders. Once you understand the theoretical concepts, you can move on to Class Exercises and Practice Exercises, through which you will learn key concepts.
  - Module 5 covers specific topics pertaining to different admission tests such as XAT, NMAT, SNAP, CMAT, etc.

**Comprehensive Classroom Sessions**

Our classroom sessions (for Offline and Online students) are aligned with your Courseware, helping you develop a strong conceptual understanding of the subject matter. In your classroom/live online sessions, your mentors will discuss and solve the questions from Class Exercises. In case you have any doubts, you can watch the video solutions under the class exercise section in your myIMS portal.
