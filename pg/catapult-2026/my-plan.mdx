myPlans are one-on-one Mentorship sessions with IMS mentors. The following are the 3 major reasons why one should take the 1-1 myPLAN sessions

1. Personalized attention: Your mentor will focus on your individual needs and goals.
2. Expert advice: Your mentor will share their knowledge and experience to help you improve your test preparation skills.
3. Motivation: Your mentor will support and motivate you to stay on track.

Based on the program enrolled, you would get a specific number of one-on-one myPlan sessions (Classroom: 5, Live Online & SLP: 3)

Depending on the stage of preparation you are in, we have listed 4 types of myPlans that you can choose from

1. **Study Plan**: This myPLAN should be taken during the early stages of preparation and after the first three modules are over for a midway check. The objective of this myPlan is to check if you are on track and what action needs to be taken to bring you back on track. You can also discuss how you should study and your weekly study schedule.
2. **SimCAT analysis**: Once you have attempted at least two SimCATs, you should use this myPlan. Here, you can discuss how to improve your scores, time management, or any other challenges you might be facing in SimCATs.
3. **B-School Application Strategy**: You should discuss applying to various colleges and appearing for various exams based on your scores in the SimCATs. The idea is to have a few ambitious colleges, a few realistic, and a few as a backup.
4. **ADMAT strategy**: ADMATs are different from SimCATs. Therefore, you should use this myPlan once you have attempted at least two ADMATs. Here, you can discuss how to improve your scores, time management, or any other challenges you might be facing in different types of ADMATs.
