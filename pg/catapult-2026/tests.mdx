Module Tests help you benchmark your performance by testing your conceptual knowledge in all three sections (QA, DILR, and VARC).

The 75 minute Module tests are to be taken at the end of every module.

Below are the different Simulated CAT (SimCAT) available to you

- **SimCAT Zero:** SIMCAT Zero is the test to be given with a zero level of preparation. This test will provide you with an idea of the test pattern and what is assessed in the test and give you a fair understanding of where you stand vis-a-vis competition.
- **Pre-SimCAT:** Set of 3 tests to be administered like a SimCAT (and like the actual CAT), but of an easy level of difficulty. This helps you to get ready for the season of SimCATs
- **Proctored SimCATs**: 20 Tests
  - Take the SimCAT within a window, preferably in the time slots of the actual test so you can prepare your body and mind for the actual test
  - Every Proctored SimCAT taken within the window gives you an All-India Rank and Percentile
  - Each question has a Video solution that will help you figure out the best way to solve the question
  - The Revise with AI feature will help you practice similar questions and get better at test-taking (a feature available only for QA and DILR)
- **Take Home SimCATs**: 20 Tests
  - Same level of difficulty as the Proctored SimCATs
  - You can take these tests any time
  - All take-homes SimCATs will have Percentile and detailed Analytics report
