title: "masterclasses"
---

The Masterclass series is designed as a Reviser series to take a student from 80 to the 90 Percentile mark. The Masterclass series has sessions covering all important question types & concepts that appear on the CAT and ADMATs. For maximum benefit, one should attend Masterclasses after they have studied concepts & practiced questions on the relevant topics.

Masterclasses consist of two parts:

- Class Exercise: Available on myIMS in a question-wise format. Students should attempt all the questions in each exercise in a time-bound manner. After one attempts the test, they can check the Video Solutions provided for the best approach to solve the question.

- Practice Exercise: The practice exercises will be available on myIMS with text solutions. Pre-recorded video solutions will not be provided. Instead, the solutions will be covered through a live online batch.

The Masterclass Series covering these Practice Exercises will tentatively commence from August 2026 onwards. Students enrolled for CAT’26 Live Online, Elite & SLP programs can attend the sessions online on myIMS.

Students enrolled in the CAT’26 Classroom program can attend either online or offline. Recordings of the online Masterclass will also be available on myIMS portal.
