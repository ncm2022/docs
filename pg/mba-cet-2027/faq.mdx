#### When will I receive my courseware?

- The courseware will be dispatched from the Head Office to your mailing address. You can expect to receive it tentatively within 7 working days from the date of enrollment. In the meantime, you can log into the myIMS portal and explore the available content there till you receive your courseware.

<Danger>
  Please Note:

  - If the amount paid is = 40% of the total course fee: Part A (Modules 0,1 and 2) is dispatched & Part B (Modules 3,4 and 5) is dispatched once the remaining course fee is paid.

  - If the amount paid is =100% of the total course fee: All Modules are dispatched.
</Danger>

#### I am still waiting to receive the courseware, what should I do?

- You should receive your courseware within 7 working days (excluding weekends and public holidays) from the date of your enrollment.
- We also send a **WhatsApp** message to your registered mobile number with the material **dispatch date**, consignment contents, the tracking ID, and the URL of the logistics company. These details  will help you track the live location of your courseware, giving you a sense of control over the delivery process.
- We will send you another WhatsApp message once your books have been delivered and we receive confirmation from the logistics company.On rare occasions, if you haven't received the study material, we request you to check with your watchman/security guard, family members, or neighbors whether they have received the courseware on your behalf.
- If you are sure the material has not been delivered within 7 working days, please submit a ticket on the [Help Desk](https://support.imsindia.com/support/tickets/new) with a detailed description. We will immediately look into the matter and resolve it as soon as possible.

#### **What are the different books that I will receive after enrolling in the course**

- We will dispatch your courseware based on the fees paid in the following parts:
  - Part A: Booklets - Module 0, 1 , 2, Grammar, Wordlist
  - Part B: Booklets - Module 3, 4, 5

#### Which videos do I refer to before and after my classes (For Classroom & Live Online students)?

- Before a scheduled class you must refer to the Pre-Class videos & solve the Pre-Class Quizzes if any. The access path is _myIMS → Prepare → Module → Topic → Pre-Class Video Playlist_
- After a class is completed you can solve all the questions again and watch the Video solutions provided for each question in the Class Exercise for revision. The access path is: _myIMS \> Prepare \> Module \> Topic \> Class Exercise Recap \> View Answer \> Video Solution_

#### Do All Class Exercises have Video Solutions along with text solutions?

- Yes, every question in the Class Exercise will have a video solution. To access the video solution you should check out the Class Recap of the topic. Here the Questions are grouped together in a playlist and there is a detailed video solution for each question. We will release the Class Recaps for the modules in a phased manner.

#### Do all Practice Exercises have Video solutions along with text solutions?

- The questions under “Practice Exercise (Basic)” and the Practice Sets do not have any Video solutions. These questions only have text solutions. The questions under Practice Exercises of each topics have Video solutions along with text solutions.

#### What is “Revise with AI”?

- “Revise with AI” also called “AI powered adaptive questions” is a revolutionary way to practice the questions. This feature is available for **QA & DILR sections** in the **Class Exercise** and **Practice Exercise** sections in the myIMS portal and **The Proctored SimCATs.**

- This option will be visible only once the student "Submits" the test, be it the Class Exercise or Practice Exercise for QA and DILR or the Proctored SimCATs.
- Once the test is submitted, when you use the "Revise with AI" option, a query will go to our servers to generate questions similar to the ones you just attempted. Irrespective of your attempt being correct or not, you can request for more questions to be generated.
- To the students who are looking for additional questions to practice of similar type this is a great feature. Many IMS students have used this feature to get consistently high scores in the SimCATs and also in the actual exams.

#### How do I attend my live classes (for Live Online students)?

- Enter the Password as **live@ims**
- Enter the same email ID as registered with IMS

#### How do I book one-on-one myPlan Sessions with a Mentor?

- myPlans are one-on-one Mentorship sessions with IMS mentors. Based on the program enrolled, you would get a specific number of one-on-one myPlan sessions
- Go to myIMS \> myPage \> myPlan \> Book Slot
- Upon booking, you will receive an email with the meeting link.
- Also, the "Join Now" button will be available **5 minutes before** your session starts, allowing you to connect with your mentor effortlessly.
